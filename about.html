About Computer Science

Computer Science is the study of computers and the processes that make them function. It focuses on programming, algorithms, data management, artificial intelligence, computer systems, and the development of software that solves real-world problems. As technology becomes more essential in modern life, Computer Science plays a vital role in shaping industries, improving communication, supporting businesses, and creating innovative digital solutions. Through this field, students and professionals learn how to design, build, and improve technologies that make everyday tasks faster, smarter, and more efficient. Computer Science is not just about using computers,it is about understanding how they work and creating new ways to use technology to benefit society.
